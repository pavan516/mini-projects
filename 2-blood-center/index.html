Information About Blood

    People Come in all different shapes,sizes and with different blood types. However, for a small percentage of the population, finding someone else with the same blood type is difficult. So, we are trying to gather all blood donors, blood banks at one place in our Blood Bank.

    Blood Types

                                                                               Although all blood is made of the same basic elements, but not all blood is same. In fact, there are eight different common blood types,(O+,O-,A+,A-,B+,B-,AB+,AB-) which are determined by the presence or absence of certain antigens. Since some antigens can trigger a patient's immune system to attack the transfused blood, safe blood transfusions depend on careful blood typing and cross-matching. The vast majority of blood types fall into one of the major ABO groups.

    Who Can Give Blood

    Most people can give blood.if you:
    => Are fit and healthy
    => Weigh over 7 stone 12 lbs or 50kg
    => Are aged between 17 and 66 (or 70 if you have given blood before)
    => Are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years.
    => Men can give blood every 3 months and women can give blood every 4 months.

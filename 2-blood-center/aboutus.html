ABOUT BLOOD CENTER


STARTUP BloodCenter- Platform For Users To Contact The Donors Directly At No Cost -THE PORTAL BLOODCENTER IS IN A QUEST TO PROVIDE THE PATIENTS BLOOD AT THE REQUIRED TIME AND AT FREE OF COST


                                                    Well, there are a lot of people who are in need of something at each and every instance of their lives. But health is something all a person cares when he is not well. There are a lot a cases where many people breathe their last due to unavailability of donors. Here the blood center is striving to solve people’s great need.

                                                    Blood center is a social service platform where the victims can avail the desired blood group at almost free of cost. It directly provides interface to the victim and the donor itself unlike the usual blood bank where they collect and store it.

THE EUREKA MOMENT FOR BLOODCENTER

                                                    Pavan Kumar is the founder and CEO of BloodCenter and has been exceptional in providing blood to the victims ever since its exception. He says that when he met with a disaster and was in need of blood very badly he faced a lot of difficulties in getting the blood and one doesn’t know the candidness of the blood they are offering.

                                                    Finally a donor stepped forward to donate him blood thus saving him from this and this led him to have the thought that he would never make any one suffer because of what he has suffered and this was how BloodCenter was born. BloodCenter is tied up with a lot of hospitals in and around Hyderabad and is serving people at its best.

FUTURE PLANS

                                                    Pavan is of the opinion that as his startup is a social platform providing services to victims all around the city it is going to grow leaps and bounds in a few years as there is awareness among people that donating blood is good for health and it produces fresh blood after you donate. They are planning to extend it to different cities in the coming years. It is totally bootstrapped and is looking for funds to extend their services.
